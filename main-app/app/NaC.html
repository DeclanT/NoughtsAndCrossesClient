Noughts & Crosses

How to Play:

Many people are familiar with the rules of noughts and crosses, the rules are very simple and straightforward and they are as follows:

Conditions of Victory

To win, all one player must have is three noughts or three crosses in a row these can either be: in a vertical line, horizontal line or diagonally arranged.

Movement

Players may choose to place a nought or cross in any unoccupied location within the game board. Each player must only take one turn at a time.
